Hello, and welcome to my academic website😀

I am currently pursuing my MPhil in Artificial Intelligence at [HKUST(GZ)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/), working under the supervision of [Prof. Hu Xuming](https://xuminghu.github.io/). I received my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from [CUPK](https://www.cupk.edu.cn/). Over the past three years, I have actively sought research opportunities in AI and am currently collaborating with [Prof. Yang Zhou](https://homepage.zhouyang.me/).

My recent research centers on the security and reliability of Large Language Models, with a particular interest in LLM Security, generative AI, and software engineering. I am broadly interested in understanding how modern foundation models can be made more trustworthy, interpretable, and aligned with human values, and the evaluation of code and reasoning capabilities in LLMs. My work has also extended to real-world applications, including fine-grained reward modeling for medical LLMs and benchmarking efforts on Chinese medical language understanding.
